About Morning Karaoke

No one owns morning karaoke, but we (see further down for "Founders") invented the particular iteration that is presented here. Morning Karaoke is a very nice activity for people who would like to have a nice time, and for others who like to sing karaoke in the morning. If you are curious about how you can organize a Morning Karaoke event, press "How to Organize a Morning Karaoke event" in the menu.
